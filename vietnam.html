Places to Visit

Hanoi
The capital city of Vietnam, renowned for its centuries-old architecture, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant street life.
Ho Chi Minh
A bustling southern metropolis, is known for its dynamic urban lifestyle, historic landmarks, and delicious street food.
Hạ Long Bay
A UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its emerald waters & thousands of limestone islands topped with rainforests.
Sa Pa
A picturesque town, celebrated for its stunning terraced rice fields, misty mountains, and vibrant local cultures.
Hue
Vietnam's former imperial capital, is known for its historic monuments and the majestic Hue Citadel.
Cao Bằng
A mountainous province in northern Vietnam, known for its stunning natural landscapes & the spectacular Ban Gioc Waterfall.

Must try food

Pho
Phở is a traditional Vietnamese soup consisting of broth, rice noodles, herbs, and meat, usually beef or chicken.
Bánh Mì
A Vietnamese sandwich featuring a crispy baguette filled with meats, vegetables, and savory condiments.
Gỏi cuốn
Buckwheat noodles, served chilled or in a hot broth.
Bánh xèo
Vietnamese savory pancakes made with rice flour, turmeric, and coconut milk, filled with shrimp, pork, and bean sprouts.
Mì Quảng
A turmeric-infused rice noodles, pork or shrimp, herbs, and a rich broth, often garnished with peanuts and fresh vegetables.
Bánh bao vac
Thick wheat noodles in broth, simple and satisfying.
Chè ba màu
a Vietnamese three-color dessert made with layers of sweet beans, jelly, and coconut milk.

Must do things in Vietnam

Cruising in Halong Bay
Typically lasting 2 days, these cruises offer a choice of cabins, onboard meals, a wide range of amenities & activities.
Coconut Boat Ride
The coconut boat ride is a unique and scenic experience usually in rural areas surrounding Hoi An.
Golden Hand Bridge
The Golden Bridge or Cau Vang, in the Ba Na Hills of Da Nang, is famous for its sculpture of two giant hands cradling a golden bridge.
Sip Vietnamese Coffee
Found in almost every street of Vietnam, the pungent yet aromatic smell of the fresh brew is difficult to miss.
Water Puppet Show
One can witness this rare art form only when in Vietnam. At the Golden Dragon Puppet Theatre in Ho Chi Minh City.
Iconic Clay tunnel
A series of basalt & dark red clay structures showing significant milestones from Da Lat’s past & present.

Itinerary Section

  • Discover the Historic Capital

    Morning: Start your day with a visit to the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum and the Presidential Palace. Then, explore the historic Old Quarter.

    Afternoon: Visit the Temple of Literature, Vietnam's first university, and stroll around Hoan Kiem Lake.

    Evening: Enjoy the vibrant night market and sample street food in the Old Quarter. Don't miss a traditional water puppet show.

  • Experience the Natural Wonder

    Morning: Travel to Halong Bay and embark on a cruise. Enjoy the breathtaking scenery of limestone karsts and emerald waters.

    Afternoon: Explore the caves and grottoes, and take a kayak tour around the bay.

    Evening: Relax on the cruise with a seafood dinner and enjoy the sunset over the bay.

  • Delve into Imperial History

    Morning: Travel to Hue and visit the Imperial City, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

    Afternoon: Explore the Thien Mu Pagoda and the tombs of the Nguyen emperors.

    Evening: Enjoy a traditional Hue royal dinner and performance.

  • Embrace the Ancient Town

    Morning: Travel to Hoi An and take a walking tour of the ancient town, including the Japanese Covered Bridge and historic houses.

    Afternoon: Visit a local market and take a cooking class to learn about Vietnamese cuisine.

    Evening: Enjoy the lantern-lit streets and riverside cafes. Don't miss the night market for souvenirs and local treats.

  • Explore the Dynamic Metropolis

    Morning: Visit the War Remnants Museum and the Reunification Palace.

    Afternoon: Explore the bustling Ben Thanh Market and visit the Notre-Dame Cathedral Basilica of Saigon and the Saigon Central Post Office.

    Evening: Enjoy the vibrant nightlife and dine in one of the city's many rooftop bars and restaurants.

  • Experience the River Life

    Morning: Travel to the Mekong Delta and take a boat tour through the canals and visit floating markets.

    Afternoon: Explore local villages, fruit orchards, and rice paddies. Enjoy a traditional Mekong Delta lunch.

    Evening: Return to Ho Chi Minh City and relax after a day of exploration.
