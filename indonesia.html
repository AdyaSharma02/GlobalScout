Must do things in Indonesia

Sacred Monkey Sanctuary
The Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary is a nature reserve and temple complex in Ubud, Bali, home to over 1260 long-tailed macaques.
Ceking Rice Terrace
A UNESCO World Heritage site, the area is trendy during sunrise and sunset over cascading rice fields.
Campuhan Ridge Walk
This is a short hike up with a beautiful view of the sunset.
Tanah Lot Temple
Tanah Lot Temple is one of Bali's most important landmarks, famed for its unique offshore setting and sunset backdrops.
Ijen Crater
Embark on an adventurous journey to Ijen Crater, a quiet yet active volcano.
Waterbom Bali
Exciting water slides through tropical parks providing hours of fun and entertainment for the young and young at heart!

Itinerary Section

  • Explore the Capital City

    Morning: Start your day with a visit to the National Monument (Monas) for panoramic views of the city. Then, explore the National Museum to learn about Indonesia's rich history.

    Afternoon: Visit Kota Tua (Old Town) to see colonial architecture and explore the Jakarta History Museum.

    Evening: Enjoy dining and shopping in the vibrant district of Jalan Jaksa and experience Jakarta's nightlife.

  • Immerse in Javanese Culture

    Morning: Travel to Yogyakarta and visit the Borobudur Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site and the largest Buddhist temple in the world.

    Afternoon: Explore the Prambanan Temple, a magnificent Hindu temple complex.

    Evening: Stroll along Malioboro Street for shopping, street food, and local handicrafts.

  • Experience the Island Paradise

    Morning: Fly to Bali and visit the Uluwatu Temple, perched on a cliff overlooking the Indian Ocean.

    Afternoon: Relax on the beautiful beaches of Nusa Dua and enjoy water sports or a spa treatment.

    Evening: Watch the traditional Kecak dance performance at Uluwatu Temple and enjoy a seafood dinner at Jimbaran Bay.

  • Discover the Cultural Heart of Bali

    Morning: Visit the Sacred Monkey Forest Sanctuary and explore the lush greenery and playful monkeys.

    Afternoon: Explore the Ubud Art Market and visit the Puri Saren Royal Palace. Take a walk through the Tegalalang Rice Terrace.

    Evening: Enjoy a traditional Balinese dance performance and dine at one of Ubud's organic restaurants.

  • Relax on Tropical Islands

    Morning: Travel to the Gili Islands and enjoy snorkeling or diving in crystal-clear waters.

    Afternoon: Relax on the pristine beaches of Gili Trawangan, Gili Meno, or Gili Air.

    Evening: Enjoy a beachside dinner and watch the sunset over the ocean.

  • Encounter the Legendary Komodo Dragons

    Morning: Travel to Komodo Island and take a guided tour to see the famous Komodo dragons in their natural habitat.

    Afternoon: Explore the island's rugged landscapes and enjoy snorkeling in the pristine waters.

    Evening: Return to your accommodation and relax after a day of adventure.
