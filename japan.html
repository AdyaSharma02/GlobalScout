Places to Visit

Tokyo
A dazzling metropolis where futuristic innovation meets ancient tradition.
Kyoto
A timeless city where serene temples and vibrant geishas paint a picture of Japan's rich cultural heritage.
Osaka
A lively blend of culinary delights and vibrant nightlife.
Nara
Ancient temples and roaming deer in a tranquil, historic setting.
Okinawa
Known for its tropical climate, beautiful beaches, and coral reefs.
Hiroshima
Known for its Peace Memorial Park, which commemorates the 1945 atomic bombing.

Itinerary Section

  • Explore the Bustling City

    Morning: Start your day with a visit to the iconic Tokyo Tower for panoramic views of the city. Then, head to the historic Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa.

    Afternoon: Visit the Tsukiji Outer Market to indulge in fresh sushi and seafood. Later, experience the vibrant Shibuya Crossing, one of the busiest pedestrian crossings in the world.

    Evening: Enjoy shopping and dining in the trendy district of Shinjuku. Don't miss the neon lights and entertainment options in Kabukicho.

  • Immerse in Traditional Japanese Culture

    Morning: Travel to Kyoto by bullet train. Begin your exploration with a visit to the Fushimi Inari Shrine, famous for its thousands of red torii gates.

    Afternoon: Visit Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), a stunning Zen Buddhist temple covered in gold leaf. Afterwards, enjoy a traditional tea ceremony in a local tea house.

    Evening: Stroll through the historic Gion district, known for its well-preserved wooden machiya houses and traditional teahouses.

  • Discover the Vibrant City

    Morning: Explore Osaka Castle and its beautiful surrounding park. Learn about the history and significance of this iconic landmark.

    Afternoon: Take a stroll in the lively Dotonbori district, famous for its street food and colorful signage. Try local delicacies such as takoyaki (octopus balls) and okonomiyaki (savory pancakes).

    Evening: Visit the Umeda Sky Building for stunning views of the city from the floating garden observatory.

  • Reflect and Explore

    Morning: Travel to Hiroshima. Visit the Peace Memorial Park and Museum to learn about the events of World War II and pay respects at the Atomic Bomb Dome.

    Afternoon: Take a ferry to Miyajima Island, known for its iconic floating torii gate at Itsukushima Shrine. Explore the island's scenic beauty and wildlife.

    Evening: Enjoy dinner on the island, savoring local specialties such as Hiroshima-style okonomiyaki.

  • Experience Natural Beauty and History

    Morning: Travel to Nara and explore Nara Park, where you can see and feed the friendly wild deer that roam freely.

    Afternoon: Visit Todai-ji Temple, home to the giant Buddha statue (Daibutsu). Explore the surrounding temple grounds and historic structures.

    Evening: Stroll through the picturesque Naramachi district, known for its traditional merchant houses and charming atmosphere.

  • Relax and Unwind

    Morning: Travel to Hakone, a region known for its hot springs and stunning views of Mount Fuji. Start your day with a relaxing soak in an onsen.

    Afternoon: Take a scenic boat ride on Lake Ashi, offering beautiful views of the surrounding mountains and the iconic Hakone Shrine torii gate.

    Evening: Visit the Hakone Shrine and explore the charming village of Hakone. Enjoy a traditional kaiseki dinner at a local ryokan.
