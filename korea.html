Itinerary Section

  • Explore the Vibrant Capital

    Morning: Start your day with a visit to Gyeongbokgung Palace and watch the changing of the guard ceremony. Then, explore Bukchon Hanok Village for a glimpse of traditional Korean houses.

    Afternoon: Visit Insadong for traditional crafts and tea houses. Head to the bustling Myeongdong district for shopping and street food.

    Evening: Enjoy the view from N Seoul Tower and explore the lively Itaewon neighborhood for dining and nightlife.

  • Experience a Historic Site

    Morning: Take a tour to the DMZ, visiting key sites such as the Third Tunnel, Dora Observatory, and the Joint Security Area (JSA).

    Afternoon: Return to Seoul and visit the War Memorial of Korea to learn about the Korean War.

    Evening: Relax in the Hongdae district, known for its youthful vibe, street performances, and cafes.

  • Discover the Coastal City

    Morning: Travel to Busan and visit the famous Haeundae Beach. Take a stroll along the beach and enjoy the sea breeze.

    Afternoon: Explore the colorful Gamcheon Culture Village and visit the Busan Tower for panoramic views of the city.

    Evening: Enjoy fresh seafood at Jagalchi Fish Market and take a walk along Gwangalli Beach, known for its night views of the Gwangan Bridge.

  • Immerse in Historical Treasures

    Morning: Travel to Gyeongju, known as the "museum without walls." Visit Bulguksa Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

    Afternoon: Explore the Seokguram Grotto and the ancient burial mounds in Tumuli Park.

    Evening: Stroll around Anapji Pond and enjoy the beautiful illuminated scenery.

  • Relax on the Volcanic Island

    Morning: Fly to Jeju Island and visit Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak) for stunning views. Explore the nearby Haenyeo Museum to learn about the island's female divers.

    Afternoon: Visit Manjanggul Cave, one of the longest lava tubes in the world. Relax at one of Jeju's beautiful beaches, such as Hyeopjae Beach.

    Evening: Enjoy a traditional Jeju black pork barbecue for dinner and explore the vibrant Jeju City nightlife.

  • Experience Korean Traditions

    Morning: Travel to Andong and visit the Hahoe Folk Village, a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its well-preserved traditional houses.

    Afternoon: Explore the Andong Mask Museum and learn about the history and significance of Korean mask dance.

    Evening: Enjoy a traditional Andong jjimdak (braised chicken) dinner and explore the city.
